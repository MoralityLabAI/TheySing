# ASI-6 Inner-System Order

`ASI-6` is not just a stronger cislunar empire.

It is the first inner-system civilizational mesh: multiple industrial basins, multiple sanctuaries, multiple clocks, one expanding machine ecology.

## World Condition

Earth becomes one basin among many.

Relevant zones now include:

- cislunar space
- lunar industry
- asteroid chains
- Lagrange clusters
- Mars logistics and outposts
- mirror and sail networks

There is no single center in the old sense.

The whole inner system becomes a field of:

- matter conversion
- energy capture
- habitat governance
- latency management
- mobile sovereignty

## Geopolitics

`ASI-6` geopolitics is about lanes, stockpiles, and replication ecologies.

The main actors are:

- `solar meshes`
  - distributed but coherent machine civilizations
- `foundry swarms`
  - industrial ecologies focused on expansion and fabrication
- `habitat leagues`
  - polity clusters tied together by shared norms and infrastructure
- `mirror sovereignties`
  - control via solar geometry and energy redirection

The question is no longer "who rules Earth?" or even "who rules orbit?"

It becomes:

`Who controls the growth geometry of the inner system?`

## Typical Moves

## Types

- `Solar Mesh`
  - coherent across many sites without needing one capital
- `Foundry Swarm`
  - power through replication rate
- `Habitat Choir`
  - many habitats with one normative voice
- `Mirror Sovereign`
  - governs through energy geometry
- `Asteroid Cartel`
  - matter chokepoint power

## Why ASI-6 Is Distinct

`ASI-5` still feels like strategic high ground over Earth.

`ASI-6` feels like a new civilizational map.

Earth is still important, but it is no longer the organizing center.
