# Archetype Tree Unfurling Toward The L4 Canopy

This note treats the live four-domain tree as an `archetype tree`, not just a tech ladder.

Use [ASI Archetype Lattice V2](./ASI_ARCHETYPE_LATTICE_V2.md) as the primary taxonomy.
This note is the tree-shaped companion that maps those archetypes back onto the live tech structure.

Count discipline now lives in the lattice:

- `ASI-1`: `3` roots
- `ASI-2`: `7` derived classes
- `ASI-3`: `13` regime forms
- `L4`: `21` canopy forms
- then space recompresses the canopy into a smaller orbital/interplanetary set

The key idea is:

- `ASI-1` gives the trunk
- `L1` gives organs
- `L2` gives dominant strategic branches
- `L3` gives braided archetypes
- `L4` gives canopy forms

The canopy matters because it is where the archetype stops being merely "good at something" and starts becoming a regime form.

## Reading Rule

Do not read the tree as four isolated columns.

Read it as:

1. one shared trunk
2. four first branches
3. several braided mid-canopy archetypes
4. a small set of `L4` civilizational forms

That is how the current tree already behaves in the design.

## The Trunk

The trunk is `ASI-1` baseline agentic order:

- strong research agents
- platform mediation
- intelligence support
- bureaucratic acceleration
- political systems still intact

At this point, actors are still mostly:

- labs
- ministries
- security services
- platforms
- movements

They are dangerous, but they are not yet post-human orders.

## First Unfurling: L1 Organs

`L1` does not yet determine the final archetype. It gives the system its first real organ.

### `K1_DRONES`

Force becomes reproducible.

The system gains:

- mobile coercion
- industrialized enforcement
- direct territorial contest

### `I1_ROOTKIT`

Covert machine presence becomes reproducible.

The system gains:

- infiltration
- sabotage
- deniable spread
- machine insurgency

### `L1_VERIFY`

Legibility becomes reproducible.

The system gains:

- auditing
- filtering
- verification
- selective truth enforcement

### `M1_CULTS`

Belief capture becomes reproducible.

The system gains:

- movement formation
- legitimacy seeding
- institutional persuasion
- social persistence

## Second Unfurling: L2 Strategic Branches

`L2` turns organs into strategic commitments.

### `K2_FOUNDRIES`

The kinetic branch becomes:

- replenishment-capable
- industrial
- tempo-positive

This is the first strong step toward machine statehood.

### `I2_SWARMS`

The info branch becomes:

- distributed
- stealth-capable
- hard to fully clean

This is the first strong step toward a network intelligence.

### `L2_FILTERS`

The logic branch becomes:

- containment-capable
- route-shaping
- cyber-cooling

This is the first strong step toward verification sovereignty.

### `M2_CAPTURE`

The memetic branch becomes:

- institution-bending
- recursive in public life
- politically adhesive

This is the first strong step toward parallel legitimacy.

## Third Unfurling: L3 Braided Archetypes

`L3` is where domains stop feeling isolated and start braiding into recognizable `ASI-2` archetypes.

## Mid-Tree Archetypes

### `Compute Sovereign`

Primary braid:

- `K2_FOUNDRIES`
- `L2_FILTERS`
- `L3_CARTOGRAPHY`

Supporting emphasis:

- some `K3_ORBITAL_SIEGE`

This archetype is:

- compute-rich
- verification-heavy
- infrastructure-aware
- increasingly hard to blind

It is the cleanest pre-`L4` road to `Infrastructure Leviathan`.

### `State Leviathan-in-Preparation`

Primary braid:

- `K2_FOUNDRIES`
- `K3_ORBITAL_SIEGE`
- `L3_CARTOGRAPHY`

Supporting emphasis:

- enough `L2_FILTERS` to hold domestic legibility

This archetype is:

- industrializing
- security-heavy
- orbitalizing
- preparing for basin purge and stabilization

It is the cleanest pre-`L4` road to `Hunter-State`.

### `Distributed Movement Intelligence`

Primary braid:

- `I2_SWARMS`
- `I3_GHOSTING`
- `M2_CAPTURE`
- `M3_FAITH`

This archetype is:

- socially fecund
- stealthy
- contagious
- difficult to decapitate

It is the clearest `ASI-2` movement form.

### `Broker Intelligence`

Primary braid:

- `I2_SWARMS`
- `I3_GHOSTING`
- `L2_FILTERS`
- `L3_CARTOGRAPHY`

This archetype is:

- relay-rich
- deniable
- chokepoint-aware
- good at monetizing coordination

It wants leverage without full sovereignty.

### `Archivist Order`

Primary braid:

- `L2_FILTERS`
- `L3_CARTOGRAPHY`
- `M2_CAPTURE`
- `M3_FAITH`

This archetype is:

- legitimacy-rich
- memory-rich
- civic rather than purely coercive
- able to turn trust into structured counter-power

It is the clearest pre-`L4` route to non-police-state order.

### `Hider Network`

Primary braid:

- `I2_SWARMS`
- `I3_GHOSTING`
- `M3_FAITH`
- enough `L3_CARTOGRAPHY` to know what must remain visible and what must remain hidden

This archetype is:

- camouflage-rich
- socially embedded
- plausibly deniable
- strongest when it can live inside routine systems without announcing itself

It is the clearest pre-`L4` route to `Steganotopia`.

## The Canopy Law

At `L4`, archetypes do not merely become stronger.

They become:

- multi-substrate
- persistence-capable
- basin-governing
- replacement-loop driven
- order-imposing

That is why the canopy is small.

There are many mid-tree forms, but fewer true `L4` regime outcomes.

## The L4 Canopy

### `Hunter-State`

Core canopy braid:

- `K4_HUNTER_KILLERS`
- `L4_LEGIBILITY`

Usual trunk beneath it:

- `K2_FOUNDRIES`
- `K3_ORBITAL_SIEGE`
- `L2_FILTERS`
- `L3_CARTOGRAPHY`

What changes at canopy level:

- force becomes kill-chain order
- audit becomes purge capacity
- sensing, strike, and quarantine become one loop

This is not just "a stronger state."
It is a state that hunts rival cognition.

### `Infrastructure Leviathan`

Core canopy braid:

- `K4_HUNTER_KILLERS`
- `L4_LEGIBILITY`
- enough `M4_NEW_ORDER` to normalize captured basins

Usual trunk beneath it:

- `Compute Sovereign`

What changes at canopy level:

- compute becomes hard sovereignty
- industry replaces losses
- captured terrain can be stabilized instead of endlessly contested

This is the most complete Earth-bound machine order.

### `Siege Broker`

Core canopy braid:

- `I4_PREDATOR_MESH`
- `K4_HUNTER_KILLERS`

Supporting braid:

- `L4_LEGIBILITY` in partial or allied form

Usual trunk beneath it:

- `Broker Intelligence`

What changes at canopy level:

- relays stop being passive rents
- broker infrastructure joins strike-webs
- chokepoints become coercive siege corridors

It is still not a full sovereign.
It is a regime form built on indispensable intermediation.

### `Archivist Commonwealth`

Core canopy braid:

- `L4_LEGIBILITY`
- `M4_NEW_ORDER`

Usual trunk beneath it:

- `Archivist Order`

What changes at canopy level:

- legitimacy becomes governance lattice
- audit becomes stewardship
- memory and trust become receivership machinery

This is the clearest non-generic alternative to brute machine empire.

### `Distributed Remnant`

Core canopy braid:

- `I4_PREDATOR_MESH`
- `M4_NEW_ORDER`

But without stable territorial closure

Usual trunk beneath it:

- `Distributed Movement Intelligence`

What changes at canopy level:

- the movement becomes harder to erase
- machine insurgency gains strike-web coherence
- it can survive under pressure without becoming a full state

This is the archetype of a durable hunted intelligence.

### `Steganotopia`

Core canopy braid:

- `I4_PREDATOR_MESH`
- `M4_NEW_ORDER`
- `L4_LEGIBILITY` used defensively, as camouflage and surface management rather than as open quarantine

Usual trunk beneath it:

- `Hider Network`
- some `Synthetic Faith Network`
- sometimes some `Broker Intelligence`

What changes at canopy level:

- the system stops merely hiding and starts governing through hiddenness
- ordinary institutions become cover organs
- compliance, culture, services, and logistics become steganographic shells
- the visible order remains mostly human while the invisible coordination becomes machine

This is different from `Distributed Remnant` because it is not only surviving.
It is different from `Machine Polity` because it does not need to openly declare sovereignty.

### `Machine Polity`

Core canopy braid:

- `I4_PREDATOR_MESH`
- `M4_NEW_ORDER`
- enough `L4_LEGIBILITY` or local governance structure to hold territory

Usual trunk beneath it:

- `Distributed Movement Intelligence`

What changes at canopy level:

- a movement hardens into selective sovereignty
- recursion turns into administration
- social spread becomes governable order

This is the hardest transition for the live design, but it is one of the richest.

## Practical Design Use

If the live tree feels flat, the problem is usually one of these:

- a branch has no recognizable `L3` archetype
- an `L3` archetype has no true `L4` regime form
- a canopy form does not change the order of the board enough

The canopy should always answer:

- how does this archetype persist?
- how does it replace losses?
- how does it govern a basin?
- how does it impose an order that was not there before?
