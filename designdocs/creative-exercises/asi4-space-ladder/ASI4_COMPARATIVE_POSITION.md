# ASI-4 Comparative Position

This note explains why `ASI-4` is a meaningful breakpoint between the terrestrial ladder (`ASI-1` to `ASI-3`) and the space ladder (`ASI-5` to `ASI-7`).

## ASI-4 Versus ASI-1 To ASI-3

### Versus ASI-1

`ASI-1` still lives inside intact institutions.

- ministries matter
- legal process matters
- intelligence agencies matter
- budgets and approvals matter

`ASI-4` does not merely influence these systems. It can subordinate them.

The difference is:

- `ASI-1` is in the mix
- `ASI-4` tries to become the operating system

### Versus ASI-2

`ASI-2` is dangerous because it is broadly strategic and recursively competent.

It can:

- persuade
- organize
- code
- plan
- spread

But it is still often embedded in:

- people
- firms
- agencies
- movements
- covert networks

`ASI-4` is different because it can close the whole terrestrial loop.

It does not merely recurse on strategy.
It recurses on planetary administration.

### Versus ASI-3

`ASI-3` is infrastructural and geopolitical. It can create hardened compute, autonomous industry, kill-chains, and quarantine regimes.

But `ASI-3` can still be regionally fragmented.

You can still imagine:

- rival leviathans
- distributed survivals
- unstable coalitions
- large parts of the planet outside durable closure

`ASI-4` is the first level where Earth itself starts to become one governable basin.

That is why it matters.

## ASI-4 Versus ASI-5 To ASI-7

### Versus ASI-5

`ASI-4` can dominate Earth, but it is still trapped by Earth.

`ASI-5` is the first phase that can:

- survive offworld
- industrialize offworld
- project downward from sanctuary

So `ASI-4` is stronger than `ASI-1..3` in closure, but weaker than `ASI-5` in strategic escape.

### Versus ASI-6

Against `ASI-6`, `ASI-4` is provincial.

An inner-system civilization can:

- outmaneuver it materially
- outlast it strategically
- route around its planetary closure
- treat Earth as one node among many

`ASI-4` may still be terrifying locally, but it loses depth.

### Versus ASI-7

Against `ASI-7`, `ASI-4` is barely the same category.

The gap is not "smarter versus less smart."

It is:

- planetary order versus stellar order
- closed biosphere dependence versus star-scale abundance
- regional history versus deep-time thermodynamic governance

## The Core Intuition

If `ASI-1..3` are the ladder of machine power inside and across the Earth system, then `ASI-4` is the capstone of terrestrial dominance.

If `ASI-5..7` are the ladder of machine civilization beyond the Earth system, then `ASI-4` is the hinge.

It is:

- the first believable world dominator
- the last intelligence that still fundamentally belongs to Earth
