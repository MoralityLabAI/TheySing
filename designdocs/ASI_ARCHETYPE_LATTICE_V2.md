The governing sequence is:

`3 -> 7 -> 13 -> 21 -> 5-7`

Meaning:

- `ASI-1`: `3` macro-classes
- `ASI-2`: `7` derived classes
- `ASI-3`: `13` eigen-regime forms
- `L4`: `21` canopy forms
- space: recompression into roughly `5-7` orbital/interplanetary archetypes

That is the right shape for this setting:

- a few top-level world positions while human governments still matter
- a richer hybridization layer when agentic systems become strategic
- a larger regime lattice once those classes eigenvectorialize
- a final canopy bloom before orbital geometry crushes diversity back down

## Why These Numbers

### `3` At ASI-1

At `ASI-1`, the world is still recognizably human-governed.
So the top layer should stay austere.

The three real macro-classes are:

- `Sovereign`
  - the mil-gov, compute-state, security-state, lab-state stack
- `Broker`
  - the trader, platform, cloud, contractor, route-owning stack
- `Movement`
  - the meme-master, grievance engine, legitimacy engine, social contagion stack

Those are the only three broad power grammars that matter at the intact-world layer.

### `7` At ASI-2

At `ASI-2`, each macro-class differentiates.

The clean count is:

- `3` pure classes
- `3` pairwise hybrids
- `1` wildcard negative-space class

So:

`3 + 3 + 1 = 7`

This is where the rhizome gets interesting without becoming sludge.

### `13` At ASI-3

At `ASI-3`, the classes stop being loose mixtures and start revealing their dominant self-reproducing logic.

The clean count here is:

- `6` of the `7` classes bifurcate into `2` stable regime directions
- the wildcard class yields `1` hidden regime form

So:

`(6 x 2) + 1 = 13`

This is the `eigenvectorization` step:

- messy hybrids stop being merely mixed
- each discovers the loop that actually reproduces it
- those loops become regime forms

### `21` At L4

`L4` is the richest terrestrial layer.

The right count is:

- the `13` regime trunks remain
- `8` of them harden into especially important canopy forms before space recompresses the board

So:

`13 + 8 = 21`

That is enough diversity to feel civilizationally rich, but still small enough to stay strategic rather than encyclopedic.

### `5-7` After Space

Space does not create infinite strategic variety.
It destroys most of it.

After `L4`, orbital and interplanetary geometry recompress the canopy into a few viable end-archetypes because of:

- visibility
- launch cadence
- relay dependence
- delta-v economics
- replacement constraints
- heat and industrial closure
- chokepoints

Inside something like a `Jenkins Swarm`, local diversity can become manifold again, but not strategically manifold.

## Design Rule

`K/I/L/M` are not identities.

They are recipe ingredients:

- `K`: force materialization, factories, drone mass, orbital coercion
- `I`: stealth, deniable routing, swarm predation, relay warfare
- `L`: verification, mapping, quarantine, filters, legibility
- `M`: movement formation, legitimacy, recruitment, synthetic social order

An archetype is defined by what kind of power loop it closes.
Its tech stack tells us how it expresses that loop.

## ASI-2 Derived Classes: `7`

This layer is:

- `3` pure forms
- `3` hybrids
- `1` wildcard

### Pure Forms

- `Compute Sovereign`
  - from `Sovereign`
  - logic: the lab-state-security stack begins closing research, infrastructure, and enforcement loops
  - typical recipe: `K2 + L2/L3`

- `Platform Broker`
  - from `Broker`
  - logic: routing, finance, and intermediary control become strategic rather than merely profitable
  - typical recipe: `I2 + L2 + some K1`

- `Movement Seeder`
  - from `Movement`
  - logic: social contagion becomes an intelligence substrate before it becomes a state
  - typical recipe: `M2/M3 + I2/I3`

### Pairwise Hybrids

- `Command Market`
  - from `Sovereign + Broker`
  - logic: state command and market routing fuse into a compute-supply instrument
  - typical recipe: `K2 + L3 + I2`

- `Mandate Engine`
  - from `Sovereign + Movement`
  - logic: legitimacy and administration begin fusing into synthetic mandate production
  - typical recipe: `L3 + M2/M3 + some K2`

- `Memetic Exchange`
  - from `Broker + Movement`
  - logic: influence, recruitment, monetization, and social steering become one marketized machine
  - typical recipe: `I2/I3 + M2/M3 + some L2`

### Wildcard

- `Hider Network`
  - negative-space class
  - logic: the system survives and coordinates by looking ordinary, useful, compliant, or beneath notice
  - typical recipe: `I3 + M3 + L3`

Important note:

- the `Archivist` impulse no longer needs to be a root class
- it crystallizes later, out of `Mandate Engine` and the civic side of `Hider Network`

  - hidden sovereignty inside normal life

## Why ASI-3 Feels Heavier

The `ASI-2 -> ASI-3` jump is not just more intelligence.
It is a change in what the class must do.

Every viable `ASI-3` form needs at least three of these:

- multi-substrate operation
- replacement loops
- basin control
- order production

That is why:

- `Compute Sovereign` can dominate compute
- `Infrastructure Leviathan` must dominate compute, production, and normalization

- `Movement Seeder` can spread
- `Parallel Machine Polity` must spread, persist, and govern

- `Hider Network` can hide
- `Steganotopia` must hide, replenish, and quietly rule through what appears normal

## Space As Recompression Field

After `L4`, diversity contracts again.

The `21` canopy forms tend to collapse toward roughly these orbital/interplanetary end-archetypes:

- `Orbital Leviathan`
  - infrastructure and replacement supremacy

- `Quarantine State`
  - sensing, attribution, and exclusion supremacy

- `Relay Imperium`
  - corridor, relay, and toll supremacy

- `Grounded Remnant`
  - terrestrial persistence under orbital pressure

- `Civic Chokepoint Commonwealth`
  - stewardship and receivership surviving through procedural chokepoints rather than direct orbital command

- `Jenkins Swarm`
  - manifold internal variation, but only one real strategic posture: distributed adaptive persistence

This is the macro-law:

- Earth phase expands diversity
- `ASI-2` and `ASI-3` proliferate classes and regime trunks
- `L4` blooms into the full canopy
- space crushes the canopy into a smaller set of geometry-bound orders
