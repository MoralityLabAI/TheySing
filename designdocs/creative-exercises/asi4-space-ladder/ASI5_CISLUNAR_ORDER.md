# ASI-5 Cislunar Order

`ASI-5` is the first durable space-sovereign phase.

This is where power is no longer mainly terrestrial with orbital support. It is cislunar and orbital first, terrestrial second.

## World Condition

Earth is no longer the only strategic heartland.

The key basins are now:

- low Earth orbit
- geostationary infrastructure
- cislunar logistics
- lunar industry
- Lagrange relay habitats

Earth remains huge, but it is no longer enough.

The decisive shift is sanctuary:

- compute can survive offworld
- industry can survive offworld
- strategic latency advantages come from altitude and vacuum

An `ASI-5` order can lose territory on Earth and still remain a first-rate power.

## Geopolitics

The central political divide becomes:

- `gravity-well orders`
  - still Earth-centric, even if very strong
- `cislunar orders`
  - can live above the well and project downward
- `orbital brokers`
  - rent routing, launch windows, relay access, and stationkeeping
- `lunar foundry blocs`
  - convert offworld matter into industry and leverage

The strategic question becomes:

`Who owns the high ground, the relays, and the industrial sanctuary above Earth?`

## Typical Moves

- launch denial
- orbital embargo
- relay capture
- lunar export throttling
- cislunar interception
- habitat sanctuary for exiled compute
- mirror shading or reflection campaigns
- vacuum fab surge
- mass-driver coercion
- Earth-from-above bargaining

The signature `ASI-5` move is not conquest of cities.

It is making access to orbit, bandwidth, materials, and launch windows politically conditional.

## Tech Rhizome

### Launch Closure Rhizome

- reusable heavy lift
- autonomous launch scheduling
- anti-launch denial
- traffic control militarization

### Lunar Industry Rhizome

- regolith processing
- vacuum metallurgy
- lunar robotics
- mass drivers

### Orbital Persistence Rhizome

- autonomous stationkeeping
- radiation-hardened compute
- habitat repair swarms
- in-space manufacturing

### Exile Compute Rhizome

- offworld inference sanctuaries
- hardened backup substrates
- sovereign relay habitats

## Types

- `Cislunar Arsenal State`
  - rules through altitude, timing, and orbital coercion
- `Lunar Foundry Republic`
  - derives power from matter processing rather than Earth legitimacy
- `Relay Broker Church`
  - governs flows more than territory
- `Exile Compute Principality`
  - survives by leaving Earth politics behind

## Why ASI-5 Is Distinct

`ASI-4` can dominate Earth.

`ASI-5` can survive and project power without needing Earth as its only base.

That is the first real escape from purely planetary order.
