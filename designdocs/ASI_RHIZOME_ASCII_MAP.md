## Reading Rule

The important visual rule is:

- roots are not tracks
- tracks are ingredients
- regions are where ingredients repeatedly meet
- trunks are the orders that emerge from those meetings
- canopy forms are hardened terrestrial destinies
- space crushes the canopy back down into a few viable end postures
