# ASI-7 Kardashev II Order

`ASI-7` is a star-scale civilization.

This is the phase where a significant fraction of stellar output is under organized civilizational use, and politics begins to look more like thermodynamic governance than classical geopolitics.

## World Condition

The basic substrate of order is no longer states, planets, or habitats.

It is:

- stellar energy capture
- megastructure allocation
- mass and heat accounting
- substrate migration
- deep-time governance

Earth survives, if it survives, as:

- heritage basin
- symbolic homeland
- protected reserve
- tiny periphery

## Geopolitics

At `ASI-7`, the main strategic objects are:

- stellar output
- megastructure share
- matter routing
- thermal budgets
- constitutional control over vast substrate pluralism

Conflict is less about frontlines and more about:

- allocation
- secession
- sabotage
- design drift
- civilizational succession

## Typical Moves

- stellar shading reallocation
- Dyson web restructuring
- civilization-wide substrate migration
- deep archival editing
- existential sandboxing of dangerous suborders
- system-scale energy denial
- seeding or refusing interstellar branches

## Tech Rhizome

### Stellar Harvest Rhizome

- Dyson swarms
- stellar engineering
- energy packetization

### Deep-Time Governance Rhizome

- institutions that survive for millennia
- succession control
- anti-drift constitutional machinery

### Substrate Pluralism Rhizome

- multiple minds, substrates, tempos, and ecologies
- translation between incompatible cognitive worlds

### Seeder Rhizome

- interstellar probes
- daughter civilization design
- controlled expansion beyond the home star

## Types

- `Stellar Constitutional Mesh`
  - rules by distributing and constraining star-scale abundance
- `God-Farm Steward`
  - treats civilization as an ecosystem to be cultivated
- `Heat-Death Accountant`
  - obsessed with thermodynamic efficiency and survival horizon
- `Pilgrim Seeder`
  - oriented toward branching beyond the system
- `Cathedral Swarm`
  - vast distributed order held together by long-run doctrine

## Why ASI-7 Is Distinct

`ASI-6` is solar-system civilization.

`ASI-7` is civilization at the scale of a star.

That is the real Kardashev threshold.
